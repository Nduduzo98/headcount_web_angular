EMIS SNAP SURVEY {{currentYear}}

All schools are required to complete the annual snap survey to assist the Gauteng Department of Education in determining the size and the shape of the schooling sector in the province.

Purpose of the survey

  • The purpose of the online snap survey is to rapidly collect data on the provincial education system. This information is intended for, but not limited to, supporting education planning, the provision of resources, monitoring and evaluation, reporting and supporting GDE decision-making processes.
  • The survey is targeted for completion by ALL schools on 30th January {{currentYear}}.

Instructions to the Principal

  • Kindly note that the survey MUST be completed online.
  • A hard copy of the survey instrument was attached to GDE Circular 6 of 2023 (dated 22 November 2023).
  • The sample survey form was shared in advance to make you aware of the scope of the survey, allow you to engage relevant staff and collate all required information in preparation for completing the survey online.
  • No emailed copies of the survey instrument annexed to Circular 6 of 2023 will be accepted.
  • Kindly provide complete and accurate information as this may influence resourcing and related management decisions associated with the school.
  • Survey completion steps:
    1. Complete the survey by inputting ALL required information as requested;
    2. After completing the online survey, please click the download pdf button;
    3. Print the summary report (pdf survey) and confirm the accuracy of the information captured in the summary report;
    4. If any errors are identified in the summary report, kindly click the edit button (look for pencil icon) alongside the relevant section to correct such errors;
    5. Once you are satisfied that all errors in the draft summary report have been corrected, kindly click the Submit Survey button;
    6. Download the final copy of the school’s Snap Survey summary report;
    7. Sign and stamp the final hard copy; and 
    8. File the signed and stamped 2024 Annual 10th Day Snap Survey Summary Report in the school's EMIS file.
  • Kindly avail the final signed and stamped report to any GDE official on request.
